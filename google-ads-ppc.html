What Is Google Ads PPC?

Google Ads PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is the world's most powerful online advertising platform. Your ads appear at the very top of Google search results when potential customers search for your products or services. You pay only when someone clicks — meaning every rupee goes directly toward attracting high-intent customers who are actively looking for what you offer.

Google Ads Campaign Types

We manage every type of Google Ads campaign to match your business goals.

01

Google Search Ads

Text ads that appear at the top of Google when customers search for your keywords. Keyword research, ad copywriting, bid management, Quality Score optimization, ad extensions (sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets), and negative keyword management.

02

Google Display Ads

Visual banner ads across millions of websites in the Google Display Network. Responsive display ads, custom audience targeting, placement targeting, contextual targeting, and brand awareness campaigns that reach new audiences at scale.

03

Google Shopping Ads

Product listings with images, prices, ratings, and store name directly in Google search results. Google Merchant Center setup, product feed optimization, Smart Shopping campaigns, Performance Max, and bid strategy management for e-commerce stores.

04

YouTube Video Ads

Video advertising on the world's second-largest search engine. Skippable in-stream ads, bumper ads, discovery ads, and masthead ads. Audience targeting by interests, demographics, and remarketing lists for video campaigns that build brand awareness and drive action.

05

Google Remarketing

Re-engage website visitors with targeted ads as they browse other websites, watch YouTube, or use Gmail. Dynamic remarketing, RLSA (Remarketing Lists for Search Ads), and customer match campaigns that bring back visitors and close more sales.

06

Conversion Tracking & Analytics

Google Ads conversion tracking, Google Analytics 4 integration, call tracking, form tracking, e-commerce revenue tracking, and custom dashboards. We ensure every lead, call, and sale is accurately measured and attributed.
