What Is Content Marketing?

Content marketing is the strategic approach of creating and distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience — and ultimately drive profitable customer action. It includes blog articles, videos, email newsletters, social media content, infographics, whitepapers, and more.
