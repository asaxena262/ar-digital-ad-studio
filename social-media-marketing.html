What Is Social Media Marketing?

Social Media Marketing (SMM) is the strategic use of social media platforms — Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and Twitter — to promote your brand, engage with your target audience, drive website traffic, and generate leads or sales. In today's digital-first world, your customers are spending hours on social media every day — and your business needs to be where they are.
