What Are Facebook & Instagram Ads?

Facebook & Instagram Ads (Meta Ads) are paid advertising campaigns that run across Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Meta Audience Network. They allow you to reach precisely targeted audiences based on demographics, interests, behaviours, and location — making them one of the most powerful tools for lead generation, sales, and brand awareness.
